I'm...

22 years old and from New Delhi, India. My journey into design started in an unexpected way. Before this, I played cricket for five years. One day, a friend mentioned he was learning graphic design. At the time, I didn’t know what that was, so I asked, "What's that?"

That simple question set me on a new path. I started exploring graphic design and soon discovered UX design. It’s all about making things look good and work smoothly to solve people's problems creatively. It reminded me of how I used to (and still do) eagerly wait for Android updates.

Now, my goal is clear. I want to be a designer who makes life easier for people by solving everyday problems through design. I aspire to create products that are simple and easy for everyone to use.
